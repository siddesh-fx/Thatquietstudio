About the studio

Quiet on set,
loud in render.

ThatQuietStudio builds real-time architectural experiences in Unreal Engine 5 — the kind clients can walk through, not just look at. We work with architects, developers, real estate teams and interior designers who need a project to feel real before a single brick is laid, through interactive walkthroughs, digital twins, pixel streaming and cinematic presentation.

Who this is for

Built For Your Side of the Table

Architects

Present a design intent that's still in flux — swap materials, test lighting scenarios, and walk a client through a space before construction drawings are final.

Developers

Sell units off-plan with a walkthrough buyers can actually explore, instead of a static render gallery they scroll past in ten seconds.

Real Estate Teams

Share a pixel-streamed link straight from a listing page — no install, no app, just a browser tab that lets a buyer tour the property remotely.

Interior Designers

Let a client toggle finishes, furniture layouts and lighting moods in real time, so the "what if we tried..." conversation happens before anything is built.

How it works

From Brief to Build

A straight pipeline from first call to a walkthrough your client can actually step into.

PROC_01

Brief & Scope

We go through drawings, references and the outcome you need — then lock scope, deliverables and timeline before any modeling starts.

PROC_02

Blockout & Assets

Massing, layout and camera paths get blocked out in-engine early, alongside asset modeling, so you're approving the real space, not a mood board.

PROC_03

Lighting & Materials

Lumen lighting passes, material customization, and day/night cycles get layered in, with review builds shared at each milestone.

PROC_04

Optimize & Deliver

Final optimization pass for target hardware, then packaged as a walkthrough build, pixel-streamed link, or rendered cinematic — your call.
